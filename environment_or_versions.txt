Example software environment
- MATLAB R2023a
- SPM
- DPABI / DPARSF
- GRETNA
- BrainNet Viewer
- Python 3.x
  - Pillow
  - NumPy
  - pandas
  - matplotlib
- R 4.4.2 (optional downstream statistics outside this package)

Local paths and installation-specific settings should be configured by the user.
Raw ADNI imaging and clinical data are not redistributed in this repository.
